# 🔍 User Research

## Overview

Before designing Shuttle Solutions, we sought to understand how Rhodes University students currently arrange long-distance transportation and identify the challenges they face throughout the booking process.

The research focused on uncovering user behaviours, frustrations, motivations, and expectations to ensure that every design decision was grounded in real user needs rather than assumptions.

---

# Research Objectives

The study aimed to answer the following questions:

- How do students currently book shuttle transportation?
- What difficulties do they experience?
- What factors influence their booking decisions?
- How can technology improve the overall experience?

---

# Research Methods

## User Interviews

Semi-structured interviews were conducted with Rhodes University students who regularly travel between Makhanda and surrounding cities.

Participants were asked about:

- Current booking methods
- Positive and negative travel experiences
- Payment preferences
- Trust in shuttle providers
- Desired improvements

---

## Needfinding

The team analysed the complete user journey from searching for transportation to arriving at the destination.

This helped identify pain points at every stage of the experience.

### Journey Stages

1. Looking for a shuttle
2. Booking
3. Waiting
4. Boarding
5. Travelling
6. Arriving

# Key Findings

## Users Need

- Convenient booking
- Trustworthy providers
- Reliable transportation
- Affordable pricing
- Simple payment methods
- Comfortable travel
- Transparent driver ratings

---

## Key Insights

### Convenience

Students prefer fast booking experiences that minimise effort.

### Trust

Recommendations from friends strongly influence booking decisions.

### Transparency

Students want clear pricing and reliable information before committing to a booking.

### Safety

Confidence in the driver is just as important as the vehicle itself.

### Technology

Students expect modern digital tools to simplify transportation planning.

---

# Design Opportunities

The research identified several opportunities for improving the existing booking experience.

- Reduce the number of communication channels required to book a shuttle.
- Build trust through verified driver ratings.
- Simplify booking and payment.
- Improve pricing transparency.
- Reduce uncertainty throughout the travel journey.

---

# Research Takeaways

The research demonstrated that transportation challenges extend beyond simply finding a shuttle. Students value confidence, convenience, affordability, and trust throughout the entire booking process.

These findings informed every stage of the design process that followed.
