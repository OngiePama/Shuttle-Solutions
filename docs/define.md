# 🧠 Define

## Overview

After analysing the research findings, the next step was to synthesise user insights into clear design opportunities.

This phase transformed raw interview data into actionable design goals through personas, Point-of-View statements, and How Might We questions.

## Maya Thompson

University Student

### Goals

- Book transport quickly
- Feel safe while travelling
- Stay within budget
- Travel comfortably

### Frustrations

- Searching Facebook repeatedly
- Unclear pricing
- Slow communication
- Difficulty trusting drivers

---

# Point of View

**Rhodes University students travelling long distances need a transportation platform that is trustworthy, convenient, and affordable because existing booking methods are fragmented, time-consuming, and unreliable.**

---

# How Might We...

The team reframed user problems into design opportunities.

Examples included:

- How might we increase trust in shuttle drivers?
- How might we simplify booking?
- How might we improve payment flexibility?
- How might we reduce travel anxiety?

---

# Prioritised Opportunities

The following opportunities were selected for further exploration:

- Driver rating system
- Flexible payment options
- Improved travel experience inside the vehicle

---

# Design Direction

The Define phase established a clear focus on designing a solution that prioritised trust, convenience, affordability, and ease of use.
